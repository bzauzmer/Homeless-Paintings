Homeless Paintings of the Italian Renaissance
Jason Mahr, Linda Song, and Ben Zauzmer

Art historian and Harvard graduate Bernard Berenson donated over 11,000 photographs of "homeless" paintings to Harvard upon his death. These paintings have been lost, stolen, destroyed in World War II, etc. The goal of this project is to visualize the collection, and perhaps even to help viewers find some of the paintings by filtering through the interactive data and using the Reverse Google Image Search link on the right side to hunt for lost art.
